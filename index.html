Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Data Scraper & Exporter ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Chrome browser extension.

Data Collection

The extension accesses the following data:

  • Website content: Visible text, tables, lists, and product grids from the active webpage — only when you click the "Scan Page Data" button. This data is processed locally and is never transmitted externally.
  • Local storage: Your license activation status and export count are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device.

Data Usage

All data processing occurs locally within your browser. We do not:

  • Collect or store any personal information (PII)
  • Track your browsing history
  • Monitor your activity across websites
  • Collect location data

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to any third parties. All scraped data remains on your local machine and is only exported when you choose to download it.

Third-Party Services

The extension uses Ko-fi for payment processing. Ko-fi's privacy policy governs any payment-related data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
